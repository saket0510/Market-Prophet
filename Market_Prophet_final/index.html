Welcome to Market Prophet

Market Prophet is an interactive visual analysis tool utilizing machine learning to predict S&P stock market trends. Leveraging the power of D3.js for data visualization, we aim to provide a seamless and intuitive experience for users to interpret complex stock market data.

To get started, select a stock by clicking any of the icons on the left. The graph will display the stock's historical performance as well as our machine learning model's future predictions.

Use your cursor to hover over specific data points on the graph for more detailed information. Each company logo will load the respective company's stock performance over the year.

Click anywhere to start interacting!

The stock data was acquired using yfinance in python
